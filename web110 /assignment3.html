The second activity I have completed in this module was using the Wayback Machine website. I have heard about this website before, but I have never had a chance to explore it. I began my search by first going through the sample websites shown on the front page of this site. It is very interesting to see how so many website layouts have changed drastically over five years or more. While making those observations, I came to the conclusion that almost every website today is very plain, and even though a programmer has made the changes, the primary influence has been done by the marketing department, as they are the ones to make those changes. After completing my brief analysis of the website's main page, I went ahead and looked at pages that I have personally used in the past, such as Gmail or YouTube. It is very interesting to see how, even in 2018, these websites went through so many changes.
